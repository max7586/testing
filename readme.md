# Requirements Traceability Matrix (RTM)

This document maps the business requirements for the "Managing Performance Rating and Award" workflow to their associated test cases. The RTM is divided into four sections covering different rating processes.

---

## 1. Performance Appraisal Plan Cover Sheet Process

| **Requirement ID** | **Requirement Description**                                                                                                                                                                           | **Test Case(s)**       | **Comments**                                                                                              |
|--------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| RQ-CS-001          | The system shall allow the **Rating Official** to select the appropriate cover sheet and assign the form to the employee or the entire business unit.                                                  | TC-CS-001, TC-CS-002   | Verifies cover sheet selection and assignment functionality.                                             |
| RQ-CS-002          | The system shall allow the **Rating Official** to submit the form to the **Approving Official**.                                                                                                      | TC-CS-003              | Ensures submission flow is triggered correctly.                                                          |
| RQ-CS-003          | The **Approving Official** shall review the form and either **sign and approve** or **disapprove** it, returning it to the **Rating Official** for correction.                                     | TC-CS-004, TC-CS-005   | Tests both approval and disapproval flows, including correct routing.                                      |
| RQ-CS-004          | If disapproved, the **Rating Official** shall be able to **make corrections** and **re-submit** the form to the **Approving Official** for re-evaluation.                                             | TC-CS-006              | Checks that the system supports correction and re-submission cycles.                                       |
| RQ-CS-005          | The **Rating Official** shall perform the final **sign-off**, approve, and submit after receiving approval from the **Approving Official**.                                                           | TC-CS-007              | Validates the final sign-off and submission steps.                                                         |
| RQ-CS-006          | The **Examiner** receives an **email notification** once the form is submitted for final approval.                                                                                                   | TC-CS-008              | Confirms email trigger and notification content.                                                           |
| RQ-CS-007          | The **Examiner** shall either **sign and accept** the form or be **bypassed** by the **Rating Official** with a valid reason.                                                                        | TC-CS-009, TC-CS-010   | Tests the Examiner sign-off flow and the bypass mechanism with reason capture.                             |

## 2. Midyear Rating Process

| **Requirement ID** | **Requirement Description**                                                                                                                                                         | **Test Case(s)**       | **Comments**                                                                                      |
|--------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| RQ-MY-001          | The **Rating Official** shall initiate the **midyear rating** process.                                                                                                              | TC-MY-001, TC-MY-002   | Verifies that the user can start a midyear rating cycle.                                          |
| RQ-MY-002          | The **Rating Official** may optionally **add a review**, add initials if needed, and submit the form to the **Approving Official**.                                                   | TC-MY-003              | Checks optional fields and submission flow.                                                       |
| RQ-MY-003          | The **Approving Official** shall review the midyear rating and either **approve** or **disapprove** the form, returning it to the **Rating Official** for correction.             | TC-MY-004, TC-MY-005   | Ensures correct handling of approval/disapproval scenarios.                                       |
| RQ-MY-004          | If disapproved, the **Rating Official** shall **make necessary corrections**, add initials if needed, and **re-submit** to the **Approving Official**.                            | TC-MY-006              | Validates the correction and re-submission functionality.                                         |
| RQ-MY-005          | The **Rating Official** shall **approve** and submit the final midyear rating after the **Approving Official**’s approval.                                                             | TC-MY-007              | Covers the final approval flow from the Rating Official.                                          |
| RQ-MY-006          | The **Examiner** receives an **email notification** once the final midyear rating is submitted.                                                                                       | TC-MY-008              | Verifies email triggers for midyear finalization.                                                 |
| RQ-MY-007          | The **Examiner** shall either **sign and accept** the midyear rating or be **bypassed** by the **Rating Official** with a valid reason.                                             | TC-MY-009, TC-MY-010   | Tests the Examiner sign-off flow and bypass logic.                                                |
